Ms. Incredible - Electronic Press Kit

Born in Los Angeles and raised all over California, TaLea Monet aka Ms. Incredible is a nomadic story-teller who has been rhyming since she can remember. She is currently residing in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Learn More

TaLea Monet is a dynamic artist, MC, and mother. She is extremely passionate and lyrical, with content to inspire, motivate, tell stories and entertain.

In 2014, she released her first mixtape entitled 'Birth of a New Breed', which was not only an amazing work of art, but also promoted unity and harmony within the family structure. Her track, "Never Meant to Hurt You", was super creative in that it was a love song to herself, in which she implies that in order to love others, she must first love herself. In the song, "Birth of a New Breed", she talks about creation and analogizes birth pains and contractions with the ups and downs of life in an ingenious display of lyrical prowess.

After releasingThe Wounded Healer EP back in December of 2018, TaLea used the following two years as a cocooning period for the anticipated release of her sophomore EP titled "Battle of GETtysberg", scheduled to drop in early 2021. She describes the project as "an all-purpose vibe to get you through your quarantine woes". After hearing "Get By" and "Get It In", the first two singles released from the forthcoming EP, one can only ponder what we'll "get" next.
